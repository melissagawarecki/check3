Attractions

Welcome to Minnetonka the perfect destination for natural beauty and exciting nightlife!

Lake Minnetonka

Minnetonka shares it's namesake with a stunning lake covers over 22 square miles.
It's popular for sailing, boating, and fishing.
Discover a little New England in the Midwest.

Ridgedale Mall

The Ridgedale mall features around 140 stores including JCPenney, Nordstrom,
Macy's, and Swavorski.
This mall is the go to place for all your shopping needs.

Big Thrill Factory

The Big Thrill Factory is the perfect place to go with your kiddos.
The Big Thrill Factory has a number of attractions including bowling, trampoline, mini golf,
a climbing wall, and laser tag, just to name a few.

General Store of Minnetonka

Founded in 1984, The General Store features over 15,000 square feet of
unique gifts, home decor, and clothing.
Stop by the store off of Highway 7.

Unmapped Brewing Company

Unmapped Brewing Co. was founded by Minnetonka natives JD and Megan Park.
Unampped has a beer for everyone (as long as you're 21).
